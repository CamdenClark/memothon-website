Get Started

  1. Enable the Memothon Custom GPT
  2. Generate a random UUID at UUID Generator (no signup required)
  3. Enter your UUID as the API key when prompted (this identifies you to the system)
Launch Memothon

Remember everything you learn

Memothon is a custom GPT that remembers what you learn and helps you review it at the optimal time to improve retention.

Learn with Memothon

When you talk with Memothon, we automatically identify and store the concepts you're learning. Our system tracks your progress and understanding, creating a personalized knowledge base that grows with you.

Review Later

Simply ask Memothon to review concepts that are awaiting review. The system uses scientifically proven spaced repetition techniques to present information at the optimal time for memory retention and long-term learning.
